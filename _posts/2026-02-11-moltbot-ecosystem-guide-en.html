Technical Architecture: The Power of Gateway Pattern

OpenClaw adopts the Gateway Architecture, which is key to its rapid expansion across multiple platforms:

  1. Core Engine: AI reasoning and decision-making hub
  2. Gateway Layer: Connects various messaging platforms and social networks
  3. Plugin System: Allows developers to extend functionality
  4. Self-Hosting Support: Users can deploy their own instances locally or in the cloud
